### Niche Strategy Advantage
By specializing in a specific market or niche, you can:
- **Charge premium prices** (specialized solutions cost more)
- **Reduce competition** (most competitors are generic)
- **Build stronger brand** ("The plugin for Greek eshops" vs "Just another WooCommerce plugin")
- **Win faster** (easier to dominate a niche than compete globally)
